Treasure Hunting and Digging

Well well, it’s your lucky day because you have the chance to dig for Solcoin treasure!


For each task you complete on Gleam, you will earn 1 treasure dig. Each treasure dig will give you the chance to dig up a winning Solcoin. All treasure digging will take place using our Discord bot (more info to come).


25 random treasure hunters will dig up a Solcoin and win. Each of the 25 hunters will win 8 SOL (200 SOL total) for digging up a Solcoin. Here comes the fun part! Each Solcoin that is dug up will also give the Solcoin NFT owner a prize of 20 SOL each (25 winners).


Example: If a treasure hunter digs up #510 then that treasure hunter will win 8 SOL and the owner of #510 will also win 20 SOL.

Prizing

  1. 300 SOL will be sent to the minter of the 1 of 1 Genesis Solcoin!
  2. 200 SOL (8 SOL each) will be sent to the 25 winning Solcoin treasure diggers!
  3. 500 SOL (20 SOL each) will be sent to the 25 Solcoin owners of the winning treasure digs!

Layers

Each Solcoin has from 6 to 14 layers. Solcoins are generated from 240 attributes. Some attributes will only be available on 1 Solcoin.

Price

The minting cost of each Solcoin will be 0.6 SOL.
